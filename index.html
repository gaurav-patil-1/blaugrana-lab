Barcelona’s identity is built on style, youth development, and a deep connection to Catalan culture. Icons like Johan Cruyff, Xavi, Iniesta, and Messi have defined generations with their artistry and success. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

Barcelona has won UEFA Champions League (5), La Liga (28+), FIFA Club World Cup (3), Copa del Rey (32+), UEFA Super Cup (5), and many historic trophies.
Messi joined Barcelona’s La Masia academy as a teen, debuted for the first team in 2004, and became the club’s greatest legend by breaking countless records.
“Més que un club” reflects Barcelona’s belief in identity, culture, and community — not just football success.
